Our History

We are a family project, sharing our love for pastry and life's sweetest things. All our recipes are made with first quality ingredients and our best intentions. We want all of you enjoy our products. This is why we take special care of our choices and work to improve our quality every day!

It all started November 2014. We enjoyed baking delicious bread for our family and friends, so we turned it into a hobby. One day, my wife and I decided to turn to social media to see where it would take us. Who would have known, our phones would be ringing off the hook and orders would come rolling in. Baking from home and maintaining full time jobs, we realized it was time to follow our dreams. With the continued love and support or our clients, we decided to open our Bakery on June 2015. Here we stand, providing you the most delicious treats made with love and appreciation for our customers.
